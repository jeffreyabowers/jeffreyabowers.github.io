About Me

Jeffrey Bowers is a visual designer, strategist, layout designer, but most importantly, a valuable asset on any design team. His technical knowledge combined with attentive attitude makes him a high-tier worker and collaborator. His ability to compromise for a final decision based on a multitude of heavy factors solidifies his decision-making skills in the workplace. On the brink of graduating from Arizona State University, his decision in choosing a BS degree in Graphics Information Technology is calculated. Based on his origins and past experiences in high school, he would ultimately gain a wide variety of skill that would lead him to this decision. Ultimately, Jeffrey would capitalize on these skills and transform them into an industry standard format.
